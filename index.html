📚 DEADLOCK CONDITIONS
1. Mutual Exclusion: Resources cannot be shared simultaneously.
2. Hold & Wait: Process holds resources while waiting for more.
3. No Preemption: Resources cannot be forcibly taken.
4. Circular Wait: Circular chain of processes each waiting for the next.
Prevention: Eliminate at least one of the four conditions.
Avoidance: Banker's Algorithm — ensure safe state always.
Detection: Periodically check RAG for cycles.
Recovery: Kill process or preempt resource.
